Get to know me!

Through my interests of figuring, analyzing and tinkering, i caught myself up in the world of computers and naturally decided to continue on this path.

Despite being a hard-working student, i also have other hobbies and interests in my freetime. I'm really passionate about musics, especially the heavier genres. I also play in a band, as the bassist.

Other than that i also love working out and engaging in other social activities.
