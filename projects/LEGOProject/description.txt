The Water Analysis Robot was an idea my group got after discussing around the world's current
"Sustainable Development Goals" given by the UN. We quickly started talking about modern
worlds water problem, which is often brushed under the carpet.<br>
Regardless of how much comfort the people in the most industrialized countries live, are
there many places on earth struggling on a daily basis to survive.
<br>
The intention behind this project was making an automized robot capable of simple analysis,
that could declare if an source of liquid is safe to consume.
